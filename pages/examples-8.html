Examples for Chapter 8

We have provided the audio files for the waveform illustrations in this chapter in AIFF format, which you should be able to open in any audio editing application, so you can examine the waveform while listening to the sound. Except where we have noted, the recordings are in stereo, so you will see two channels, not the single one illustrated in the book. For copyright reasons, we cannot supply the audio for Figure 8.5. We suggest that you look at the waveform for a recording of singing to which you have the rights.
