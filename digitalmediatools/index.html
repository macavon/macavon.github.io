Digital Media Tools, 3rd Edition

Digital Media Tools by Nigel and Jenny Chapman is a practical introductory tutorial text for courses and individuals using Adobe software. This completely revised and re-illustrated 3rd edition was published in 2007 and covers the CS3 versions of Adobe's programs Bridge, Photoshop, Illustrator, Flash and Dreamweaver.

This book is largely obsolete, as it describes old versions of Adobe's software. Some general principles remain valid, however. These pages are an archive of its support site, but much of the material is not relevant. There may be broken links to projects and products that are no longer supported.
